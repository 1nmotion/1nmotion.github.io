Being the person that just watches movies for fun, it shouldn’t be a surprise that I thought of the theme BEST PICTURE for this crossword, the 36-Across revealer. Spending a lot of time digging through the thesaurus helped me to come up with the rest of the themed answers. Take “French art heist subject”. The answer is MONA LISA (24-Across), stolen once from the Louvre, ultimately becoming the world’s most famous painting, or a “best picture”.
For PARASITE (51-Across), I had no idea how to clue this until I remembered framed, my favourite daily game, which would then lend to the film winning the Best Picture award at the 92nd Academy Awards, an appropriate theme answer.
